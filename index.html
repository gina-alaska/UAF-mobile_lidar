UAF-mobile lidar

UAF campus mobile LiDAR collection planning

September 6th - 1PM - West Ridge Research Building Parking Lot

James Young from Aerometric, author of http://bloglidar.com/ and the AeroMetric/Maser Consulting team's mobile LiDAR unit will be available for an introduction to Mobile LiDAR followed by a data collection of an upper campus loop (see below for proposed loop)

The Demo of the LiDAR unit is to educate interested parties on the technology, how it works and what can be derived from the data. The unit will be used to collect data and provide that data to UAF and interested parties so that it can be evaluated. We encourage questions and concerns about the technology to be brought up during this demo.

Possible 1 mile West Ridge Route

map of alternative ~3 mile route of upper campus

Data delivered would be fairly raw - and not geo referenced relatively accurate but more work would be required to properly pin it to the ground.

This is offered by Aerometric to UAF for free as a sample research product for evaluation.

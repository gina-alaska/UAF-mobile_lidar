UAF Mobile Lidar

Possible route for a mobile LiDAR collection on UAF campus by Aerometric

Data delivered would be fairly raw - and not geo referenced relatively accurate but more work would be required to properly pin it to the ground.

This is offered by Aerometric to UAF for free as a sample research product for evaluation.
